Top 10 restaurant in Ma On Shan

The conditions were set to the number of favorites greater than 1000, while the number of likes was more than ten times the number of dislikes to filter out the most highly rated group of restaurants in the Ma On Shan area on openrice.

Restaurants of different categories in Ma On Shan

Among the filtered restaurants, I grouped them with different categories of cuisines and counted the average prices of well-reviewed restaurants with different categories. Overall, Chinese restaurants are more affordable, while Taiwanese, Japanese and Southeast Asian cuisines are the most expensive. At the same time, Chinese, Hong Kong, Western, and Japanese received more positive reviews and were more popular in the Ma On Shan area.
